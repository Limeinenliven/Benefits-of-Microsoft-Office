## ❓ FAQ

<details>
<summary><b>What's the difference between Office and Microsoft 365?</b></summary>

Office is the core suite of apps. Microsoft 365 includes Office apps plus cloud services and subscriptions.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Is Microsoft Office free?</b></summary>

Free web versions are available. Full desktop apps require a subscription.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I use Office on Mac?</b></summary>

Yes! Microsoft Office is fully compatible with Mac.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What's the best Office app?</b></summary>

Word for documents, Excel for data, PowerPoint for presentations, Outlook for email.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How much does Office cost?</b></summary>

Microsoft 365 Personal: $6.99/month, Family: $9.99/month. Business plans vary.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I use Office offline?</b></summary>

Yes! Full desktop apps work offline. Sync changes when online.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Is Office safe?</b></summary>

Yes. Office includes enterprise-grade security features.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I collaborate with Office?</b></summary>

Yes! Real-time co-authoring and seamless sharing.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Does Office work on mobile?</b></summary>

Yes! Full apps for iOS and Android.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What's OneDrive?</b></summary>

Microsoft's cloud storage. Seamlessly integrated with Office.

</details>
